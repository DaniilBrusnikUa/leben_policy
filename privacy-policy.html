1. Overview

This app ("Leben in Deutschland: 460 Fragen") is developed by Daniil Brusnik (Ukraine). We are committed to protecting your privacy.

2. Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data on our servers. All data (favorites, progress, settings) is stored locally on your device only using AsyncStorage and is never sent anywhere.

3. Third-Party Services — Google AdMob

This app displays ads provided by Google AdMob. Google may collect and use data for advertising purposes, including:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID / IDFA)
  • IP address
  • Usage data

This data is processed by Google in accordance with their Privacy Policy.

You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking → disable for this app

4. App Tracking Transparency (iOS)

On iOS 14 and above, we request permission to track your activity via Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework before any advertising data is collected. You may deny this permission and the app will continue to function normally with non-personalized ads.

5. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
