## 1. Overview

This app ("Leben in Deutschland: 460 Fragen") is developed by Daniil Brusnik (Ukraine). We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## 2. Data We Collect

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data** on our servers.

All data (favorites, progress, settings) is stored **locally on your device only** using AsyncStorage and is never sent anywhere.

## 3. Third-Party Services — Google AdMob

This app displays ads provided by **Google AdMob**. Google may collect and use data for advertising purposes, including:
- Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
- IP address
- Usage data

This data is processed by Google in accordance with their Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings:
- **Android**: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
- **iOS**: Settings → Privacy → Tracking → disable for this app
